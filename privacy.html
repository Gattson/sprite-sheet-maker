Enkava

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 24, 2026

The short version Enkava runs entirely in your web browser. There are no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking. Your artwork stays on your device — we never see it, because there is no server for it to go to. The only time money or personal details change hands is if you choose to leave a voluntary tip, which is handled by Stripe, not by us.

Enkava (“Enkava,” “the Service”) is a browser-based drawing, vector, and animation tool operated by Calivo LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”), a limited liability company based in South Carolina, USA. This policy explains what information is and isn’t involved when you use Enkava at enkava.com.

1. Information we collect

From you, directly: none. Enkava has no accounts, no sign-up, and no login. We don’t ask for your name, email, or any personal information to use the app.

Your artwork and projects stay on your device. Everything you draw is created and held locally in your browser. When you save a project, it is downloaded as a file to your own computer. Your artwork is never uploaded to us or to any server — Enkava is delivered as static files that run on your machine, and we operate no servers that could receive your work.

Local browser storage. Enkava uses your browser’s localStorage to remember your own settings between visits — things like your editor preferences, custom brushes, and color palettes. This data lives only in your browser, is not transmitted to us or anyone else, and you can clear it at any time through your browser’s settings.

2. Cookies, analytics & tracking

Enkava sets no cookies and uses no analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking of any kind. We do not profile you, and we do not sell or share personal information — because we don’t collect any to begin with. The local browser storage described above is used solely to remember your settings and is never shared.

3. Hosting and server logs

The Enkava website is served by GitHub Pages (a service of GitHub, Inc.). Like essentially every website host, GitHub may automatically record standard technical information — such as your IP address, browser type, and which files were requested — for security and to operate the service. This information is collected and handled by GitHub under GitHub’s Privacy Statement. We do not use it to track or identify you.

4. Voluntary tips (payments)

If you choose to leave a voluntary tip through the “Chip in” button, you are taken to a checkout page hosted by Stripe, Inc. Any payment details you enter — such as your card number — are collected and processed by Stripe under Stripe’s Privacy Policy. We never receive or store your full card number. We receive only limited transaction information from Stripe (for example, the amount and any name or email you provided to Stripe) so we can confirm the payment. Tipping is entirely optional; you can use everything in Enkava without ever making a payment.

5. Links to other services

Enkava links to a few third-party services, such as our community Discord and the Stripe checkout above. When you follow those links you leave enkava.com, and your activity there is governed by that service’s own privacy policy, not this one.

6. Children’s privacy

Enkava is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information (for example, through a tip), contact us and we will address it.

7. Your choices and rights

Because we don’t collect or store personal data on our own servers, there is generally nothing for us to look up, correct, or delete. The local data Enkava stores is entirely under your control in your own browser and can be cleared at any time. For any records tied to a tip you made, those are held by Stripe as the payment processor; contact us and we’ll help where we can.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll revise the “Last updated” date at the top, and for material changes we’ll note it on the site. Continuing to use Enkava after an update means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

Questions about this policy or your privacy? Email intuneplayer@gmail.com.
