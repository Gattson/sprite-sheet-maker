One canvas · the whole shelf

Everything a 2D artist juggles apps for — in one place.

No more switching between a painting app, a vector app, and an animation app. Enkava does all three, and hands the file straight to you.

Paint freely

Pressure-sensitive brushes, textures, and smudge over full-color layers with blend modes. No grid, no limits.

Or stay crisp

A classic pixel mode with palettes, tidy grids, and pixel-perfect export for sprite work.

Vector that bends

Editable vector strokes you reshape point by point — the same lines built to move when rigging arrives.

Bring it to life

Frames, onion skinning, and in-canvas playback. Watch the walk cycle move before you ever export it.

Export anywhere

PNG sprite sheets, animated GIF or APNG, video, or a ZIP of frames. Print projects come out at true physical size.

Yours to keep

It all runs on your machine — your art never leaves it. Projects save as plain files you own outright.
